# Ecosystem Overview

## IronRidge Contractors

IronRidge Contractors is a fictional mid-sized commercial contractor and field operations company based in Indianapolis, Indiana. It operates across General Contracting, Field Services, Special Projects, and Operations Support, with the usual pressure that comes from growth, coordination friction, and uneven reporting.

## The Four Demo Repositories

- `ops-visibility-demo` - leadership reporting for project health, service backlog, closeout readiness, and operational risk
- `workflow-drag-reduction-demo` - support request workflow for intake, routing, approvals, and aging visibility
- `execution-infrastructure-demo` - execution tracker for commitments, blockers, ownership, and weekly follow-through
- `contractor-ops-system-demo` - field operations tracker for issues, escalation, punch items, and closeout readiness

## How This Repository Fits

This repository is the leadership view of the ecosystem.

It turns shared operational records into a dashboard-style reporting layer that helps leaders review what is happening across the business without rebuilding the picture by hand.

## Shared Company Context

All four repos use the same project catalog, employee roster, divisions, regions, and operating language so they feel like connected parts of one internal environment rather than separate demos.

## Portfolio Intent

This repo is meant to read quickly: believable business context, readable sample data, sharp documentation, and one strong visual artifact that explains the idea without overcomplicating it.
