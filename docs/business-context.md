# Business Context

IronRidge Contractors has outgrown the reporting habits that worked when the business was smaller.

The company now spans multiple divisions, regions, project types, and support functions. Important updates still move through spreadsheets, inboxes, calls, and meeting notes, which means the full picture is usually available but rarely fast.

## Current Realities

- project updates are often managed in separate PM-owned trackers
- service work is not always rolled up the same way across teams
- closeout pressure tends to become obvious later than anyone wants
- field issues do not always show up clearly in leadership reporting
- action follow-through can affect delivery before it is visible at the reporting level

## Why Leadership Cares

Leadership does not need more dashboards for the sake of dashboards.

They need quick, credible answers to a short list of questions:
- which projects need attention right now
- where service backlog is building
- which closeout items are slipping
- where risk is starting to accumulate before it becomes a bigger problem

That is the gap this repo is meant to make visible.
