# KPI Dictionary

## Active Projects

Count of projects currently in `Active` status.

## Projects At Risk

Count of projects where `RiskStatus` is `At Risk`.

## Open Field Issues

Count of field issues still open at the time of reporting.

## Closeout Items Overdue

Count of closeout items with positive overdue age.

## Service Tickets Open Greater Than 7 Days

Count of service tickets still incomplete more than seven days after open date.

## Action Items Overdue

Count of action items not complete with due dates already passed.
